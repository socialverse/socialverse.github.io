the thought left in the textbox

the tress rush past me

all these scenes blurred

into a thought of you


lately, 'empty' vacated my brain

after you made it your home

my half-asleep eyes shot close


the calm in my heart

brought a warm curved string

in my empty space


one has rested and caused

disaster in this heart

but you cleaned the place

and called it home


stay in this life

and be with me

let's create the days greater

than what it used to be


don't avoid my gaze

rest in my space
