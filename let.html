let him dance in your love

how embarrassing, he smiled on your face

when his heart was in conflict with his mind

he was keeping his cool but his hands were trembling

when she mentioned her previous heart.


unexpecting soul tried to dodge the dagger -

coming his way, only to pierce his heart!

you! cruel her! he was pleasing you!

never mention your previous heart, and whisper it in his ear


your words have power over him -

he's a child dancing in your weather!

what you give gave his mood, be careful - he lives for you -

mention only your current heart, let him dance in your love
